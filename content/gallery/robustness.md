Robustness analysis inspired by Section 3.10, using the Growth+ model for
internal validity and sensitivity experiments.

![Co-movements at leads and lags](/images/gallery-robustness-1.png)

*Internal validity: cross-correlations of unemployment, productivity, price
index, and real wage with GDP across multiple seeds.*

![Impulse-response function](/images/gallery-robustness-2.png)

*AR(2) vs AR(1) impulse-response functions for the GDP cyclical component.*

![Sensitivity to credit market reach](/images/gallery-robustness-3.png)

*Sensitivity analysis: co-movement structure under different values of the
credit market reach parameter H.*

![Preferential attachment experiment](/images/gallery-robustness-4.png)

*Structural experiment comparing log GDP under loyalty-based vs random
consumer matching (Section 3.10.2, Figure 3.10).*

![Entry neutrality](/images/gallery-robustness-5.png)

*Entry neutrality: mean unemployment across three profit tax rate levels.*
