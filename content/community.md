## Related Projects — The CATS Family

The BAM model (Delli Gatti et al., 2011) has inspired a family of
implementations and extensions within the CATS (Complex Adaptive Trivial
Systems) lineage.

### BAMmodel (NetLogo)

NetLogo implementation by Platas-López et al. using the ODD protocol.

- [github.com/alexplatasl/BAMmodel](https://github.com/alexplatasl/BAMmodel)
- Platas-López et al. (2019). DOI [10.3233/FAIA190141](https://doi.org/10.3233/FAIA190141)

### ABCredit (Julia)

Developed at Banca d'Italia. Extends BAM with a two-sector supply chain.

- [github.com/bancaditalia/ABCredit.jl](https://github.com/bancaditalia/ABCredit.jl)
- Assenza, Delli Gatti & Grazzini (2015). DOI [10.1016/j.jedc.2014.07.001](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jedc.2014.07.001)

### R-MABM (Python + Julia)

Reinforcement-learning extension of ABCredit with tabular Q-learning.

- [github.com/Brusa99/R-MABM](https://github.com/Brusa99/R-MABM)
- Brusatin et al. (2024). DOI [10.1145/3677052.3698621](https://doi.org/10.1145/3677052.3698621)
